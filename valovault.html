AGENT

In Valorant, agents are unique characters with specialized abilities, divided into four roles: Duelists (offensive fighters like Jett and Phoenix), Controllers (map manipulators like Brimstone and Viper), Initiators (disruption experts like Sova and Breach), and Sentinels (defensive support like Sage and Cypher). Each agent brings a distinct tactical approach to the game, allowing for diverse strategies and team dynamics.
